This model was created as a graduation requirement for the MSc program in Environmental Sciences (Engineering Track) at Kuwait University. The model allows the user to calculate the carbon dioxide emissions for Kuwait’s energy sector (eight power plants) by inputting the fuel consumption data (published yearly by the Ministry of Electricity & Water on their official website). Furthermore, by inputting the electricity production values, you will be able to calculate Kuwait’s Aggregate Carbon Intensity ‘ACI’ for that year (CO2 emission per electrical use).

The project was titled “A comparative study of carbon dioxide emissions from power plants in Kuwait:
status quo versus 2030” and was supervised by Dr. Duaij alRukaibi from the Civil Engineering Department.

Owned and created by: Engineer / Ahmad Fouad AlSalem

About the creator:
Ahmad AlSalem graduated from the civil engineering BS program at Kuwait University in 2014. He then went on to pursue his professional career at the Ministry of Electricity & Water and helped in establishing the Private Projects Department. He completed his MSc in Environmental Sciences in 2018.
